Delete your KeepSweep account

This page explains how to delete the optional account in the KeepSweep app (by gosadu) and exactly what happens to your data.

Delete it inside the app (instant)

  1. Open KeepSweep
  2. Go to Settings (bottom right)
  3. Tap Account
  4. Tap Delete account and confirm

Deletion is immediate and permanent.

Or request deletion by email

If you no longer have the app installed, email support@johncompany.co from the email address you signed up with, with the subject "Delete my KeepSweep account". Requests are completed within 30 days.

Delete your data without closing your account

You can also delete data while keeping your KeepSweep account:

  1. Data on your phone: in the app, go to Settings > Your data > Clear local data. This wipes the stats, streak, and staged items stored on your device. Your photos are never affected.
  2. Synced data on our servers: email support@johncompany.co from your sign-up email with the subject "Delete my KeepSweep data". We erase your synced usage counters and ad-impression rows while keeping your sign-in intact, within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your sign-in (email and password)
  • All synced usage counters (items reviewed, items deleted, storage freed, per day)
  • All ad-impression log rows tied to your account

What is kept

  • Nothing on our servers. No data tied to your account is retained after deletion, and there is no additional retention period.
  • Your photos and any data stored locally on your phone are never on our servers in the first place, and deleting the account does not touch your device.
