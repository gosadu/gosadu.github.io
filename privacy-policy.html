KeepSweep Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-06-10 · Contact: support@johncompany.co

The short version

Your photos never leave your phone. KeepSweep reads your photo library locally so you can review and delete items you choose. There are no analytics SDKs, no tracking profiles, and ads are non-personalized by default.

Photos and videos

KeepSweep displays your photos and videos using your operating system's photo-library API. They are never uploaded, copied to our servers, hashed, or analyzed off-device. Deletion happens only through your operating system's own confirmation dialog. The app explicitly does not request access to photo location (EXIF GPS) data on Android.

Data stored on your phone

Per-day counters (items reviewed, items deleted, bytes freed), your streak, items you staged for deletion, your reminder preference, and ad-frequency counters. You can export all of it as JSON or erase it at any time in Settings → Your data.

Optional account

If you create an account (email + password), your per-day counters and streak are backed up to our database (hosted by Supabase), protected by row-level security so only your account can read them. No photo data is ever synced. You can delete your account and all synced data inside the app: Settings → Account → Delete account.

Ads

KeepSweep is free because it shows a small, clearly-labeled banner ad - at most 3 per day, never while you are swiping, and never before the app has actually helped you. Ads are requested from Google AdMob in non-personalized mode (no device-ID-based ad targeting by KeepSweep). In regions that require it (for example the EEA and UK), Google's consent form is shown first and your choice is respected. To serve and measure ads and prevent fraud, Google may process limited technical data such as IP address and coarse, non-identifying performance signals; see Google's privacy policy and AdMob policies.

Ad impression log

If you are signed in, each ad shown stores: the date, which slot it appeared in (home or recap), your platform (iOS/Android), and the app version - nothing about your photos, behavior, or identity beyond your account id. Signed-out usage stores ad counters only on your device.

Sponsor cards

Occasionally a directly-sponsored card may appear in the same two slots, always labeled "Sponsored" with the sponsor's name. Sponsors receive no data about you. Tapping opens their website in your browser.

What we never do

No selling or sharing of personal data. No access to contacts, location, microphone, or camera. No third-party analytics. No ad targeting based on your photos or any sensitive data. KeepSweep is not directed at children under 13.

Your choices

  • Use the app fully without an account (local mode) or try the no-permission demo.
  • Export your data: Settings → Your data → Export as JSON.
  • Erase local data: Settings → Your data → Clear local data.
  • Delete your account and synced data: Settings → Account → Delete account.
  • Control photo access anytime in system settings (or the in-app photo-access manager).
  • Where applicable, revisit ad consent: Settings → Ads & privacy → Ad privacy options.

Changes

If this policy changes, the in-app copy (Settings → About → Privacy policy) and this hosted copy are updated together, with a new effective date.
