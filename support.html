Common questions

Did KeepSweep really delete my photo?

Only if you confirmed the system dialog. Swiping left just stages an item; nothing is removed until you tap "Free up space" and your phone asks for confirmation. Recently deleted items can usually be recovered for ~30 days in your phone's Photos app (Recently Deleted / Trash).

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your sign-in and all synced stats from our servers. Your photos and local data are untouched.

How do I export or erase my data?

Settings → Your data → Export as JSON, or Clear local data.

Why am I seeing an ad?

Ads keep KeepSweep free. They're capped at 3 per day, never appear while you're swiping, and are non-personalized by default. Details: Settings → Ads & privacy.

The app can only see some of my photos

You granted partial access. Use Settings → Photo access inside KeepSweep to manage which photos are visible, or grant full access in your phone's system settings.
